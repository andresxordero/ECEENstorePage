    How it works?

    The ECEEN solar power system is designed via lightweight, flexible and high efficiency solar cells with USB voltage regulator, and solar battery special to collect solar power. Photovoltaic panels turn sunlight into electricity that can be stored in the high capacity USB battery unit.
    Whenever you need it, you can plug your device to the battery to continue using it also when it runs out of charge.
